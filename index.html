Sustainability & Fair Trade
Our mission

Our mission is to empower marginalized communities in Nepal by promoting sustainable, Fair Trade products that support both environmental protection and economic development.

Through our efforts, we aim to uplift local farmers and artisans, provide educational support, and create lasting value for communities in Nepal, all while promoting sustainability and preserving traditional craftsmanship.

  • 1 Empower Marginalized Communities
  • 2 Foster Environmental Stewardship
  • 3 Promote Ethical Consumerism
  • 4 Cultivate Partnerships for Global Impact

Product summary

  • Ecofair Dog Chew is inspired by an ancient Himalayan recipe for a hard cheese snack traditionally enjoyed by the people of the high mountains. Sourced from the remote regions of Nepal, above 15,000 feet in the Himalayan range, these chews are made using traditional methods with yak (dri) and/or cow milk. Our Dog Chews are 100% natural, with no preservatives, binding agents, or gluten—just pure, wholesome ingredients.

  • Our handcrafted items are not only a celebration of unique artistry but also represent a profound connection to communities that have long been marginalized, discriminated against, or exploited. These products are thoughtfully created by artisans from indigenous communities, leprosy-affected individuals, survivors of trafficking, and those overcoming various forms of violence.

  • we are proud to offer unique Arabica coffee grown in Nepal, a country known for its stunning Himalayan landscapes. While over 70% of the world's coffee is Arabica, Nepal's mountainous environment makes it particularly special. Arabica beans thrive in higher altitudes and cooler temperatures, making them a perfect fit for Nepal's terrain. Nepali Arabica beans are rich with a dark, nutty flavor, but what truly sets them apart is their distinct profile.
